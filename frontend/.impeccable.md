# SOFTBEE Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
Vietnamese tech shoppers, age 18–40, browsing on primarily desktop but also mobile. They compare specs, hunt deals, and want to feel they're buying from a modern, reliable platform — not a generic store. Context: seated at home or work, product research mode, trust and clarity are paramount.

### Brand Personality
**Three words**: Sharp. Precise. Electric.

The brand is SOFTBEE — a tech electronics retailer. Think less "generic e-commerce" and more "the store that takes technology seriously." Clean lines, high contrast, a sense that every detail has a purpose. Not flashy, but unmistakably confident.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Light with strong dark accents — crisp whites with deep charcoal-navy surfaces, electric amber for primary CTA
- **Typography**: Geologica (angular, technical display) paired with Geist Sans (crystal-clear body)
- **Color palette** (OKLCH-based):
  - Surface: `oklch(0.98 0.005 250)` (cool near-white)
  - Primary text: `oklch(0.14 0.02 255)` (deep charcoal-navy)
  - Accent/CTA: `oklch(0.78 0.17 75)` (electric amber — energetic, warm, distinct)
  - Muted text: `oklch(0.52 0.01 255)`
  - Border: `oklch(0.91 0.008 255)`
  - Newsletter/dark section: `oklch(0.12 0.025 260)` (very dark navy, NOT pure black)
- **Banned AI tells**: No purple-to-blue gradients, no glowing cyan, no generic card shadows everywhere
- **Motion**: Fast, purposeful — 200-250ms ease-out-quart. Hover states that feel alive but never distracting.

### Design Principles
1. **Precision over decoration** — Every visual element must earn its place. No ornaments for ornament's sake.
2. **Hierarchy through contrast** — Big/small, heavy/light, dark/light. Type scale must be dramatic enough to guide the eye instantly.
3. **Amber is sacred** — The electric amber accent appears sparingly: primary buttons, active states, key labels. Overuse kills the energy.
4. **Technology feels fast** — Interactions snap. Transitions are brief. Loading states are minimal. The UI communicates speed.
5. **Space creates trust** — Generous whitespace around product cards. Crowded = cheap. Breathe.
